# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Cooked ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information:** When you create an account, we collect your email address and create a unique user identifier.
- **Recipe Data:** Recipes you import or create, including titles, ingredients, steps, tags, and images.
- **Menu Data:** Your menu plans, cooking history, and preferences.
- **Grocery Lists:** Shopping lists generated from your menus.

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Usage Data:** How you interact with the App, including features used, recipes imported, and menus created.
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, and app version.
- **Analytics Data:** Aggregated usage statistics to improve the App (via PostHog, if enabled).

### Information from Third Parties

- **Recipe URLs:** When you import a recipe, we process the URL you provide to extract recipe information.
- **Payment Information:** If you subscribe to Pro, payment is processed by Apple through the App Store. We receive subscription status but not payment details.

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide and maintain the App
- Process and save your recipes, menus, and grocery lists
- Sync your data across devices
- Process subscription purchases
- Send important service notifications
- Improve the App based on usage patterns
- Respond to your support requests

---

## Data Storage and Security

### Where We Store Data

Your data is stored on Supabase, a cloud database service. Supabase servers are located in the United States and comply with industry-standard security practices.

### How We Protect Data

- All data is transmitted using HTTPS encryption
- Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms
- Database access is restricted and monitored
- We use Row Level Security (RLS) to ensure users can only access their own data
