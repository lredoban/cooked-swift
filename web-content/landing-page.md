## How It Works

### Step 1
**Find a recipe you want to cook**
Scrolling TikTok? See a recipe on Instagram? Found something on a food blog? Copy the link.

### Step 2
**Import it to Cooked**
Paste the URL and Cooked extracts the recipe automatically. Edit if needed, then save to your library.

### Step 3
**Add it to your menu**
Select recipes from your library to build this week's menu. Aim for 3-5 meals.

### Step 4
**Generate your grocery list**
Cooked combines all ingredients and organizes them by category. Shop, cook, enjoy.

## FAQ Section

### Q: What platforms can I import recipes from?
A: Cooked supports TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube videos, and most recipe websites. If it has a URL, we can probably import it.

### Q: Does Cooked work offline?
A: Once recipes are imported, they're available offline. You can view your menu and grocery list without an internet connection.

### Q: Can I share my grocery list?
A: Yes! Share your list as text with family members. They can help shop even without the app.

### Q: What if the recipe import isn't perfect?
A: You can edit any imported recipe before saving. Adjust ingredients, fix steps, or add notes.

### Q: Is there a web version?
A: Cooked is currently iOS-only. A web companion for shared grocery lists is coming soon.
